ABOUT

Hi, I'm Samuel Bloch, I'm a professional guide and I love Finnish nature!
I've been passionate about wild animals, and birds in particular, since I was a kid.

I come from the French Alps. I arrived in Finland as a student, and found a haven of peace that I always return to, no matter where my adventures take me. In Helsinki, we're very lucky to have spectacular natural places within easy reach, and I'm delighted to show them to you.

In the field, I am known for my relaxed but dedicated attitude. I find joy in sharing my knwoledge and enthusiasm for the natural world. I adapt to your level of experience, whether you're a novice or an experienced birder, and make sure we have a great time together.

Besides birding and guiding, I'm a story-driven wildlife and conservation photographer.
The stories I tell have people and wildlife as subjects. Through them, I want to nurture understanding, empathy and care for the natural world, for I firmly believe all things have the right to grow.

You can discover my photographic work on my website, samuelbloch.fr
